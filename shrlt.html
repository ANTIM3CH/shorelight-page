Who Is ShoreLight

About the band

SHORELIGHT is a 1 piece metalcore outfit from the bowels of East Cobb county. With roots planted deep from the realm of smooth jazz, as well as competative Scandinavian hog whistling. All the way to disheartening doom sludge dad pop. SHORELIGHT most definitely has a style all their own. From the age of 3 songwriter/performer/whistler, Austin "Big Booty Doodie" Self has been hand widdling his own instruments. Such as, the flute, trombone, 9 string guitar, the hurdy gurdy and many more. With this craftmanship and practice, as well as a strict diet of Bush's Baked Beans, he is the absolute portrait of peak physical and mental ability. All of these attributes combine in a sultry swirl of putrid yet ambrosial ambience that is sure to dance all over your tingling jiggly bits. Click to get tickets to a show near you!
